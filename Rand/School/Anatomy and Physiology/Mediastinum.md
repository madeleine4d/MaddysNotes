- Superior Mediastinum:
	- Organs: [[thymus]], [[trachea]], [[esophagus]]
    - Arteries: aortic arch, brachiocephalic trunk, left common carotid artery, left subclavian artery
    - Veins and lymphatics: superior vena cava, brachiocephalic veins, the arch of the azygos, thoracic duct
    - Nerves: left and right vagus, recurrent laryngeal, cardiac, left and right phrenic nerves[1](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[2](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[8](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[3](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)
- Anterior Mediastinum:
	- Organs: [[thymus]]
    - Arteries: internal thoracic branches
    - Veins and lymphatics: internal thoracic branches, parasternal lymph nodes
    - Nerves: none[1](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[2](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[8](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[4](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[5](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)
- Middle Mediastinum:
	- Organs: the [[heart]] and its great vessel roots, trachea and main bronchi
    - Arteries: ascending aorta, pulmonary trunk, pericardiacophrenic arteries
    - Veins and lymphatics: superior vena cava, pulmonary veins, pericardiacophrenic veins
    - Nerves: phrenic, vagus, sympathetics[1](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[2](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[8](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[9](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[5](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[6](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[7](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)
- Posterior Mediastinum:
	- Organs: [[esophagus]]
    - Arteries: descending thoracic aorta
    - Veins and lymphatics: azygos hemiazygos veins, thoracic duct
    - Nerves: vagus, splanchnic, sympathetic chain[1](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[2](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[8](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)[5](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK539819/#)
